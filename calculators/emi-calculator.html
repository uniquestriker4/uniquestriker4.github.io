EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. This EMI calculator helps you calculate your monthly EMI based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. It is useful for home loans, car loans, and personal loans.

EMI Formula

EMI is calculated using the following formula:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N − 1]

Example EMI Calculation

For a loan of ₹5,00,000 at an interest rate of 10% for 60 months, the EMI will be approximately ₹10,624.

Why EMI Calculation Is Important

Calculating EMI helps you understand your monthly financial commitment before taking a loan. It allows better budgeting and prevents financial stress.
