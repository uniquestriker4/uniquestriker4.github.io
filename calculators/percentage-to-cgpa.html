Percentage to CGPA Formula

The most commonly used formula to convert percentage into CGPA is:

CGPA = Percentage ÷ 9.5

Example Calculation

If a student has scored 76%, then the CGPA will be:
76 ÷ 9.5 = 8.0 CGPA

Why Percentage to CGPA Conversion Is Needed

Many universities, especially engineering colleges, require CGPA values for admissions, semester evaluations, internships, and placements. Converting percentage to CGPA ensures compatibility with such requirements and helps students fill academic forms correctly.

Accuracy of This Calculator

This calculator uses the standard conversion method followed by most Indian universities. However, some institutions may use different formulas, so it is always recommended to check official guidelines.
