BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement that helps determine whether a person has a healthy body weight. This BMI calculator uses your height and weight to calculate BMI and give a general health indication.

BMI Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 and above

Why BMI Matters

BMI helps identify potential health risks related to body weight. While it is not a perfect measure, it is widely used for basic health assessment.
