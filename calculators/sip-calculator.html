A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in mutual funds. This SIP calculator helps you estimate the future value of your investment based on monthly amount, expected return rate, and investment duration.

SIP Formula

SIP future value is calculated using compound interest, considering monthly investments.

Example

If you invest ₹5,000 per month for 10 years at 12% annual return, the total value will be approximately ₹11–12 lakhs.

Why SIP Is Popular

SIP promotes disciplined investing and reduces market timing risk. It is suitable for long-term financial goals like education, retirement, and wealth creation.
