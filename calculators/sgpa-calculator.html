SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) reflects a student's academic performance in a particular semester. This SGPA calculator helps students keep track of their semester results easily.

Why SGPA Is Important

SGPA contributes to overall CGPA and is used by institutions to evaluate semester-wise performance.
